The Work

Over the past year, God has opened doors for me to visit 5+ countries and several states. Everywhere I go, I'm meeting young people who are searching for something real. My prayer is that they find Jesus in those moments when their hearts are most open.

Recently I spent time at a missions school in Germany where we learned to reach young people in ways that don't look like traditional evangelism. In Dresden, we got to see young people come to know Christ and get baptized...in a bathtub we set up in a park! It was wild and beautiful.

Recent Activity

What's Happening

This next year feels big. I'm moving to Poland to live in a house with other missionaries who share the same passion for reaching young people across Europe. It's going to be our home and our base for ministry.

Here's what's exciting: I'm taking on the cooking for our team, but it's way more than just making meals. I've been thinking a lot about how food brings people together and breaks down walls. There's something about sharing a meal that makes people open up. I'm figuring out creative ways to use food to share the Gospel with the young people we're trying to reach.

We're also looking at opening a restaurant in the house. Not just as a ministry thing, but as an actual business that could fund the mission long-term. Imagine a place where young people naturally want to hang out, where we can build real relationships and share Jesus in the most organic way possible.
